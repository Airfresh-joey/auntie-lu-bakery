Our Story

A Family Recipe, Shared With Love

It started in Auntie Lu's kitchen - flour dusting every surface, the smell of fresh bread filling the house, and a promise to never rush what takes time to make right.

Every loaf we bake carries that same philosophy. We use a wild-fermented starter that's been nurtured for years, locally sourced flour, and nothing artificial. Ever.

Our bread takes 24-48 hours to make. That's not a selling point - that's just how long it takes to do it properly. Some things can't be rushed, and we think you can taste the difference.

Auntie Lu
Founder & Head Baker
