## Image/Creative Recommendations

### Photography Style
- **Lighting:** Warm, natural light (golden hour feel) - matches brand aesthetic
- **Backgrounds:** Rustic wood surfaces, marble countertops, linen napkins, San Diego beach/coastal backdrops
- **Color palette in images:** Warm tones (cream, blush, gold, warm brown) matching brand colors
- **Style:** Artisan, handcrafted feel - NOT commercial/sterile

### Creative Types to Produce
| Format | Use Case | Specs |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Single Image** | Product showcase, testimonials | 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1920 (stories) |
| **Carousel** | Product range showcase (4-6 slides) | 1080x1080 per card |
| **Video (15-30s)** | Process shots, reveals, time-lapses | 1080x1920 (Reels/Stories) |
| **Video (60s)** | Brand story, behind-the-scenes | 1080x1080 or 1080x1920 |
| **UGC-style** | Customer testimonials, unboxing | 1080x1920 (raw, authentic feel) |

### Specific Creative Ideas
1. **"The Reveal"** - Video of opening a cake pop box, close-up of the designs
2. **"Before & After"** - Dough ball to finished sourdough loaf time-lapse
3. **"The Process"** - Hands decorating cake pops, dipping, adding details
4. **"Happy Customer"** - UGC-style video of a birthday party with Auntie Lu's cake pops
5. **"San Diego Love"** - Product shots with iconic SD backdrops (Coronado Bridge, La Jolla Cove, Balboa Park)
6. **"Flat Lay"** - Overhead shot of a spread: sourdough, cake pops, cupcakes on rustic table
7. **"Split Screen"** - Left: grocery store cake. Right: Auntie Lu's custom creation.

## Retargeting Strategy

### Tier 1: Hot Leads (1-7 days since visit)
- **Audience:** Added to cart but didn't checkout, visited checkout page
- **Ad type:** Carousel showing items they viewed + urgency messaging
- **Frequency cap:** 2x/day
- **Budget allocation:** 40% of retargeting budget
- **Message:** "Your order is waiting! Complete it before your date fills up."

### Tier 2: Warm Leads (8-30 days since visit)
- **Audience:** Visited product pages, spent 60+ seconds on site, viewed 3+ pages
- **Ad type:** Testimonials, process videos, customer photos
- **Frequency cap:** 1x/day
- **Budget allocation:** 35% of retargeting budget
- **Message:** "Hundreds of San Diego families trust Auntie Lu's for their celebrations."

### Tier 3: Cool Leads (31-90 days since visit)
- **Audience:** Any website visitor, Instagram/Facebook engagers
- **Ad type:** Seasonal promotions, new product launches, brand story
- **Frequency cap:** 3x/week
- **Budget allocation:** 25% of retargeting budget
- **Message:** "New this season at Auntie Lu's!" or seasonal/holiday hooks

### Exclusions
- Always exclude past 7-day purchasers from all retargeting
- Exclude email subscribers from lead generation campaigns (they're already captured)

## Local Service Ads Setup

### Google Business Profile Optimization (Required First)
1. **Claim and verify** Google Business Profile for "Auntie Lu's Bakery"
2. **Primary category:** Bakery
3. **Secondary categories:** Cake Shop, Wedding Bakery, Custom Cake Shop
4. **Service area:** San Diego County + surrounding areas (set 50-mile radius)
5. **Photos:** Upload 20+ high-quality images (products, behind-the-scenes, storefront/pickup area)
6. **Posts:** Weekly Google Business posts featuring new products, seasonal specials
7. **Reviews:** Actively solicit Google reviews from every customer (send follow-up email with direct review link)
8. **Q&A:** Pre-populate with common questions (ordering process, lead times, pickup info)
9. **Products:** List all products with photos and prices in GBP product section
10. **Hours:** Set clear ordering/pickup hours

### Local Inventory / Local Services Approach
- Since Auntie Lu's is a home bakery with pickup, focus on **Google Business Profile optimization** rather than formal Local Service Ads (which are primarily for home services)
- Run **location-targeted search ads** with location extensions pulling from GBP
- Enable **call extensions** so mobile searchers can call directly
- Add **price extensions** showing starting prices for each product category
