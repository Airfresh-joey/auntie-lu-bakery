Phase 4 Scale & Ship Nationally (Month 4+)

The big play: frozen sourdough shipped anywhere.

The Frozen Bread Model

How it works

Bread is par-baked (80% done), flash frozen, and shipped in insulated boxes. Customer finishes baking at home in 10-15 minutes. Arrives "fresh from the oven" anywhere in the country.
