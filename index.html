Our Story

Welcome to Auntie Lu's Kitchen!

Like many great ideas, Auntie Lu's Bakery started in my home kitchen. In 2022, I discovered the ancient, meditative process of sourdough baking. The science, the patience, and the incredibly rewarding crust and crumb of a perfectly proofed loaf immediately captured my heart.

Baking sourdough quickly evolved from a hobby into a much-needed creative outlet - a way to step away from the daily grind and create something truly nourishing.

While the sourdough feeds the soul, the cake pops feed the party! I found a different kind of joy in transforming simple cake into beautifully designed, perfectly dipped pop art that brings instant smiles to any gathering.

Auntie Lu
Founder & Head Baker

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you deliver or ship?

+

Auntie Lu's Bakery is pickup only at this time! We're located in San Diego (92120) near Waring Rd and Zion Rd.

What is the pickup address?

+

Pickups are near Waring Rd and Zion Rd in San Diego, CA (92120). The exact address will be provided in your confirmation email the Monday of your scheduled pickup week.

How do cake pops come packaged?

+

Cake pops are individually wrapped with allergy info noted on the back, and packaged laying flat in a windowed pastry box by default. For an additional fee, you can upgrade to a windowed cake pop carrier that doubles as a display stand!

Do cake pops need to be refrigerated?

+

Cake pops can be stored at room temperature for up to 5 days, in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks, or frozen for up to 3 months.

Do you cater to allergies?

+

Unfortunately, I'm unable to accommodate specific food allergies. All items are made in a kitchen that handles gluten, dairy, nuts, and eggs, so treats may contain traces of these allergens.

How does payment work?

+

Full payment is required upfront to secure your order date. Once we've finalized your order details, I'll email you an invoice with multiple payment options.
