Our Story

Welcome to Auntie Lu's Kitchen

Like many great ideas, Auntie Lu's Bakery started in my home kitchen. In 2022, I discovered the ancient, meditative process of sourdough baking. The science, the patience, and the incredibly rewarding crust and crumb of a perfectly proofed loaf immediately captured my heart.

Baking sourdough quickly evolved from a hobby into a creative outlet—a way to step away from the daily grind and create something truly nourishing. The cake pops? A happy accident that turned into a massive hit!

Every item is made with genuine care, high-quality ingredients, and a touch of homemade love. You aren't just getting bread or a treat; you're getting a piece of my personal creative journey.

— Auntie Lu

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering

Do you deliver or ship?

Auntie Lu's Bakery is pickup only at this time. We're located near Waring Rd and Zion Rd in San Diego, CA (92120).

What is the pickup address?

The exact address will be provided in your confirmation email the Monday of your scheduled pickup week.

How are cake pops packaged?

Cake pops are individually wrapped with allergy info noted on the back, packaged in a windowed pastry box. Upgrade to a display carrier available!

Do cake pops need refrigeration?

Cake pops stay fresh at room temperature for up to 5 days, in the refrigerator for 2 weeks, or frozen for up to 3 months.

Do you accommodate allergies?

Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate specific food allergies. All items are made in a kitchen that handles gluten, dairy, nuts, and eggs.

How do I place an order?

Use our Contact page to request a custom order form. For bread, browse our shop and add items to your cart directly!
