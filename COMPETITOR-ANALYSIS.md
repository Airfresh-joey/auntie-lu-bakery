## SAN DIEGO MARKET OPPORTUNITIES

### Beach Wedding Cakes
- San Diego = destination wedding capital
- Beach/coastal themed cakes are HUGE
- Target: La Jolla, Coronado, Del Mar venues

### Quinceañera Market
- Large Hispanic population
- Quinceañera cakes = recurring business
- Target: Chula Vista, National City, El Cajon

### Military Market
- Multiple Navy/Marine bases
- Military weddings, retirements, promotions
- Target: Coronado, Point Loma

### Tech/Corporate
- Growing tech scene (Qualcomm, etc.)
- Corporate events, office parties
- Target: Sorrento Valley, UTC, Downtown
